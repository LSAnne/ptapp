FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s included in online coaching?

You get a personalised training plan, progress tracking, accountability check-ins, and messaging support to keep you on track.

Do I need a gym membership?

No — I’ll tailor your plan to what’s available to you, whether that’s at home, outdoors, or in a gym.

How do I submit progress photos?

Use the Client Upload page to send your photos or Google Sheets. Just paste the link and click submit!

How soon can I expect results?

Most clients feel and see progress within 4–6 weeks of consistent training, but the real win is building habits that last.

Can I ask questions between check-ins?

Yes! You’ll have access to WhatsApp or email support for questions, form checks, or motivation.
